# Production Guide

This is the practical guide for turning the voice into TikToks, Reels, X posts, and screenshots.

## Performance

The performance should feel like reporting, not acting.

Rules:

- calm face
- dry delivery
- no smile before the punch
- no "you get it?" energy
- no explaining at the end
- no big acting
- give the final line half a second of silence
- say it like the sentence is obvious and society is late

## Short Video Structure

### 6-14 Second Reel

1. On-screen text: the hook.
2. Spoken line 1: the situation.
3. Spoken line 2: the crooked interpretation.
4. End: one short closing line or silence.

Example:

> לא ברור לי מי אישר הודעות קוליות מעל דקה.  
> זה לא תקשורת, זה לקחת בן אדם בן ערובה עם כפתור פליי.  
> תשלחו תקציר.

## Visual Formats

### Face To Camera

Best for:

- one-liners
- complaint-style lines
- "I request to appeal"
- "not clear who approved"

### POV

Best for:

- car
- street
- elevator
- phone screen
- someone approaching
- checking if a person is safe to interact with

### Screenshot / Text Card

Best for:

- very short lines
- X-style jokes
- "wait, read that again" posts

### Situation Overlay

Best for:

- showing the exact scene
- text over a real moment
- turning ordinary footage into evidence

## Caption Rules

Captions should be short and not beg for engagement.

Use:

- לא נקבתי בשמות.
- הוגשה תלונה.
- בעיה מערכתית.
- אין לי פתרון, רק ראיות.
- זה לא אישי. זה ציבורי.

Avoid:

- מי מזדהה?
- תייגו חברה כזאת
- אמאלה זה כל כך אני
- בואו נדבר על זה

## Posting Rhythm

Start simple:

- 3 short posts per week
- 2 Reels/TikToks per week
- 1 raw observation dump per week for yourself, not necessarily published

After two weeks, review:

- which lines feel natural to perform
- which posts people quote back
- which comments reveal stronger angles
- which formats feel forced

## Content Batch

### 30-Minute Batch

1. Pick one topic: morning, work, messages, people, traffic.
2. Write 10 raw observations.
3. Convert 5 into formulas.
4. Choose 3.
5. Film each twice.
6. Keep the less performed take.

## Editing Rules

- Subtitles large enough to read.
- One thought per line.
- No busy visual design.
- Cut pauses before the first word.
- Keep the silence after the punch.
- If the joke needs a sound effect, it is probably not sharp enough.

## What To Track

Do not over-measure early.
Track only:

- saves
- shares
- comments that quote the line
- people saying "this is too accurate"
- whether you still like the line after posting

## Refinement Loop

Every week, sort posts into:

1. Exactly me.
2. Close but needs tightening.
3. Not me.

For each "exactly me" post, ask:

- What was the situation?
- What was the formula?
- What was the exact kind of exaggeration?
- Was it more self, people, or system?

Then make 5 siblings from the same structure.
